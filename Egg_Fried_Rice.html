Egg Fried Rice

Description

This orange chicken recipe is sticky, sweet, and delicious. It tastes similar to the orange chicken from a popular Chinese restaurant in the mall! It's easier to make than you think for delicious takeout at home.

Ingriedient

1 cup water
2 tablespoons soy sauce
½ teaspoon salt
1 cup uncooked instant rice
1 teaspoon vegetable oil
½ onion, finely chopped
½ cup green beans
1 large egg, lightly beaten
¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

Step1

Bring water, soy sauce, and salt to a boil in a medium saucepan.
Stir in rice and remove from heat. Cover and let stand for 5 minutes.

Step2

Heat oil in a medium skillet or wok over medium heat.
Sauté onions and green beans in hot oil for 2 to 3 minutes.
Pour in beaten egg and fry for 2 minutes, scrambling egg while it cooks.

Step3

Add cooked rice to egg mixture; mix well. Season with pepper.
